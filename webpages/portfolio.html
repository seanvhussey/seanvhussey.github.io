Accessibility

Everyone should be able to access and enjoy the web. Accessibility is the practice of making websites usable by as many people as possible. We traditionally think of this as being about people with disabilities, but the practice of making sites accessible also benefits other groups such as those using mobile devices, or those with slow network connections. Regardless of what ones situation is the web should be able to benefit them in some way, the closer we are to a free acessible web the more we are interconnected which is ultimately the goal of the internet, the sharing of information and ideas.
